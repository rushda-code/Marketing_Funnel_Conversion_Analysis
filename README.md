# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

---

## Problem Statement
Marketing funnels help businesses understand how potential customers move from lead generation to final deal closure. 
Identifying drop-offs and optimizing conversion rates is essential for improving marketing efficiency and return on investment.

---

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze marketing qualified leads and closed deals data to measure funnel performance, evaluate channel conversion rates, and identify opportunities to improve lead-to-customer conversion efficiency.

---

## Dataset Description
This project uses two datasets from Olist's marketing funnel process:

1. **Marketing_Qualified_Leads.csv** – Contains information about generated leads including lead origin and acquisition details.
2. **Closed_Deals.csv** – Contains information about leads that successfully converted into closed deals, including business segment and declared revenue.

By combining these datasets, the complete funnel from qualified lead to closed deal can be analyzed.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Google Colab

---

## Key Analysis Performed
- Total Marketing Qualified Leads calculation
- Total Closed Deals calculation
- Overall conversion rate measurement
- Funnel drop-off analysis
- Conversion rate by lead origin
- Business segment performance analysis
- Revenue distribution analysis of closed deals

---

## Key Insights
- A significant portion of qualified leads drop off before deal closure.
- Certain lead origins demonstrate higher conversion rates than others.
- Business segments contribute differently to closed deal performance.
- High-value revenue segments can be identified among successfully converted leads.

---

## Strategic Recommendations
- Allocate greater marketing investment toward high-converting lead sources.
- Improve follow-up strategies during early funnel stages to reduce drop-offs.
- Optimize sales focus on high-revenue business segments.
- Continuously monitor conversion performance across marketing channels.

---

## Conclusion
This analysis highlights funnel efficiency, channel effectiveness, and revenue potential. 
Optimizing high-performing acquisition channels and reducing conversion drop-offs can significantly improve marketing ROI and overall sales performance.
